💬 Local Knowledge

Curated tips — not scraped

⚠

Ferry frequency is low — missing the last return sailing means an unplanned extra night on the island.

⚠

The Blue Cave on Biševo requires a boat trip and gets extremely crowded in July and August. Book in advance or go in June or September.

✦

Stiniva Cove

Consistently rated one of Croatia's most beautiful beaches — a narrow pebble cove reached through a dramatic rock gap. Crowded at midday in summer, magical at dawn or dusk.

✦

Vis town wine bars

Vis produces some of Croatia's best indigenous wines, particularly Vugava white and Plavac Mali red. The wine bars in Vis town are unpretentious and excellent.
